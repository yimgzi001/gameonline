About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an addictive survival action game that challenges you to withstand relentless waves of monsters. As you progress, you'll collect experience gems, level up your character, and unlock powerful abilities that will help you survive even longer. The game combines simple controls with deep strategic gameplay, making it easy to learn but challenging to master.

Your character automatically attacks nearby enemies, allowing you to focus on positioning and dodging incoming threats. How to Play

1

Move Your Character

Use the arrow keys or WASD keys to move your character around the battlefield. Keep moving to avoid being overwhelmed by monsters!

2

Auto-Attack Enemies

Your character automatically attacks the nearest enemies. Position yourself strategically to maximize damage while staying safe.

3

Collect Experience Gems

Defeated enemies drop experience gems. Collect them to fill your experience bar and level up.

4

Choose Upgrades Wisely

When you level up, you'll be presented with multiple upgrade options. Choose carefully based on your playstyle and current situation.

5

Survive as Long as Possible

The game gets progressively harder. Your goal is to survive for as long as you can and achieve the highest score!

Pro Tips for Success

Keep Moving

Constant movement is key to survival. Don't stand still, or you'll quickly become surrounded.

Balance Your Build

Don't just focus on offense. Defensive upgrades and area control abilities can be lifesavers.

Learn Enemy Patterns

Different monsters have different behaviors. Understanding them helps you prioritize targets effectively.

Time Your Gem Collection

Sometimes it's better to leave gems on the ground and collect them when it's safer to do so.
